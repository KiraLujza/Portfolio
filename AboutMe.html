About Me

Hello! I'm Kira Lujza Kiss.

A beginner hobby photographer based in Hungary. Through photography and digital art, I capture moments that feel quiet, powerful, or easily overlooked. I love experimenting with different styles and techniques, always searching for new ways to grow and express myself creatively.

Every image is part of my journey, and I hope my work inspires others to find beauty in the ordinary.

My photography style is focused on atmosphere, emotion, and storytelling. I’m drawn to contrasts between light and shadow, quiet moments, and details that are often overlooked. I enjoy capturing a mix of nature, architecture, and live energy, creating images that feel both cinematic and personal. Through dark tones, strong composition, and mood-driven visuals, I aim to turn everyday scenes into something expressive and memorable.
